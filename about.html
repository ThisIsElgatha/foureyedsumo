About us

Four Eyed Sumo is more than a design firm. We’re more than a handful of top-notch, creatives who constantly churn amazing work. We are our projects. We live them, breathe them, eat them and dream about them. Some people call it obsessive. We call it life. And we wouldn’t have it any other way.

About us

We took right-brained senior creatives with left-brained seasoned strategists and mashed them together to create the ultimate integrated marketing, branding, design firm in the world with phenomenal cosmic powers! Ok, maybe no cosmic powers but definitely super ones when it comes to growing your business and delivering measurable results.

We’re a hybrid at the turning point of a crossroad of evolution – like a platypus… with a cape. Platypus Man? No, definitely not that. Even cooler.

We offer a full range of services with clients including start-ups, pro-athletes and individual entrepreneurs. No project is too large (or too tiny) as long as it has vision. Our vision. Or at least x-ray vision. Because that’s a cool superpower too.
